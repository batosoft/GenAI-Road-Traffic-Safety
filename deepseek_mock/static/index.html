About This Project

The GenAI Road Safety Application is designed to enhance road safety through two innovative AI-powered components:

Road Hazard Reporter

Using Ollama Vision models, this component analyzes images of damaged roads to:

  • Classify the type of damage (potholes, cracks, etc.)
  • Assess severity and safety impact
  • Generate structured reports for authorities
  • Contribute to early detection and repair
Traffic Fine Analyzer (Salama AI)

Powered by DeepSeek V3, this component analyzes traffic fine history to:

  • Identify patterns in driving behavior
  • Provide personalized safety tips
  • Offer educational information about violations
  • Calculate potential financial savings

Together, these components create a comprehensive solution for improving road safety, reducing accidents, and promoting responsible driving behavior.
